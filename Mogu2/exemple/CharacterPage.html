Miranda Beth Morris was the wife of Pvt. Mo Morris and a former stranded. Her husband joined the COG via Operation Lifeboat and revived a Class 3 ration from the COG Department of Conscription.[1] During her time at Jacinto City, she sent letters to Mo about the kids and how life was until Tollen and Montevado fell to Locust forces.
